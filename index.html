About the Showcase

Every year, my Game Design students create games and submit them for showcase consideration. This year, we are excited to have a couple of awesome students whose games made the cut. Now, we turn to you—members of our community—to help us judge the winner and to obtain invaluable feedback.

I think this year’s games came out awesome and I am very proud of all of our participants. Some of the games that you will be playing are the products of hundreds of hours of work. It may not look like it, but make no mistake: the process of creating even the simplest game involves a tremendous amount of hard work, persistance, and grit.

Whereas professional game designers work with hundreds of other talents artists and engineers, my students only had each other (and their teacher, of course) to confide in. I’m not going to lie. There were struggles along the way. Many cursed their computers, the inventors of Construct 2, and even their teacher too. Yet, we survived and learned a lot in the process.

Thanks for taking the time to support us!
